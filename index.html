FAQ

Questions

Is my audio sent to any server?

Never. Dictami uses OpenAI's Whisper model running entirely on your Mac. Audio is processed locally and deleted immediately after transcription. No account, no API key, no cloud.

What Mac do I need?

macOS 13 Ventura or later. Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) is recommended for the best speed. Intel Macs are supported but transcription may take a second or two longer.

Which languages does it support?

Whisper supports 50+ languages including English, Russian, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic, and many more. Just select your language in Settings.

Does it work when offline?

Yes — once the Whisper model is downloaded (a one-time step in Settings), Dictami works with no internet connection at all.

How is this different from Apple Dictation?

Apple Dictation sends audio to Apple's servers (unless you enable Enhanced Dictation, which is limited). Dictami is 100% local, adds punctuation automatically, works in more languages, and inserts text into any app via clipboard or Accessibility API — not just where the system cursor is.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked. Email support and you'll get a full refund.
